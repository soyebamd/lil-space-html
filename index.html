Peace within Reach

Research shows that constantly checking our mobile devices creates a dopamine loop, increases addictive behavior and takes us out of the moment. While you can turn off alerts and you can turn off your mobile device, you can't turn off the anxious impulse to check if anyone is trying to reach you.

LilSpace is an answering machine for the 21st century. Blocking the distraction of incoming texts and calls is not enough. LilSpace gives you the peace of mind that your selected contacts will receive a customized auto-reply text stating why you are not available and when you will be back in touch. Your selected contacts can relax and so can you.
